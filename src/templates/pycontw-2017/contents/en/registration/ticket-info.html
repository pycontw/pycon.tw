2. Everybody Pays

PyCon Taiwan 2017 is a paid conference. Everybody, including staffs and speakers, pays for attending (see PyCon: Everybody Pays).

3. Ways to Pay for Tickets

You can pay through credit card, PayPal, or ATM transfer.

4. No Refund

There will be no refund once registration is confirmed.

5. Financial Aid Plan

You can contact us for a financial aid if you feel any difficulty affording a ticket and/or other expanses attending the conference. You will need to purchase the ticket before applying. More info about applications can be found here.

6. Dietary Requirements

We provide lunch during the conference. Please let us know if you have any specific dietary requirements.

7. Contact Information Accuracy

Please provide valid means of contact (phone number and email) on purchase, so that we will be able to inform you of any important notices.

8. Ticketing Consultancy

Please kindly email to organizers[at]pycon.tw if you have any questions regarding ticket information. We will reply you as soon as possible!

9. Other Events

You will be able to verify information about tutorials (annoucement pending) and sprints through the website’s navigation menu.
