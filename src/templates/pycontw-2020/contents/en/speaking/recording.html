Recording Release

Since one of the PyCon motivation is to help Python education and advocate the use Python, by default all talks are recorded. If the speaker agrees to give permission to PyCon Taiwan, we will record, edit, and release the audio and video of the speaker's presentation to online video site such as Youtube and pyvideo.org. The recording might be also live streamed if it is technically feasible.

If you don’t want your talk being recorded, make sure you leave the recording option unchecked during the proposal submission.
